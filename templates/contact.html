Frequently Asked Questions

Is ScamShield free to use?

Yes. ScamShield is a completely free, open-source tool. There are no hidden fees or premium tiers.

How accurate are the predictions?

Our ensemble ML models achieve high accuracy on test datasets. However, predictions are probabilistic — always exercise due diligence and verify through official channels.

Do you store the job data I submit?

No. All analysis happens in real-time during your session. We do not store, log, or share any job posting data you submit for analysis.

Can I contribute to the project?

Absolutely! ScamShield is open source. Fork the repository, make improvements, and submit a pull request. We welcome contributions of all kinds.
