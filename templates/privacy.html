Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Last Updated: June 14, 2025

01 Information We Collect

ScamShield is designed with a privacy-first architecture. We are committed to minimal data collection:

  • Job Posting Data: When you submit a job posting for analysis (via manual input or URL scanning), the text is processed in real-time on our server. We do not store, log, or persist any job posting content after the analysis is complete.
  • URL Scanning: When you provide a URL for scanning, we fetch the page content server-side to extract job posting details. The fetched HTML is processed transiently and discarded after the response is sent.
  • No Account Data: ScamShield does not require account creation, login, or any form of user registration.
  • No Cookies: We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. The only client-side storage used is localStorage for your theme preference (dark/light mode).

02 How We Use Information

Any data you provide is used exclusively for the following purpose:

  • Real-Time Analysis: Job posting text is processed through our NLP classifiers, metadata analyzers, and heuristic engines to generate a trust score and risk report.
  • No Profiling: We do not build user profiles, track browsing behavior, or perform any form of behavioral analytics.
  • No Selling: We never sell, rent, or share user data with third parties for any purpose.

03 Data Retention

ScamShield operates on a zero-retention model:

  • Job posting text submitted for analysis is held in server memory only during the HTTP request lifecycle.
  • Once the analysis response is returned to your browser, all submitted data is immediately discarded.
  • Server logs may temporarily contain request metadata (IP address, timestamps) for operational debugging, but these are rotated and deleted within 7 days.

04 Third-Party Services

  • Font Awesome CDN: We load icon fonts from Font Awesome's CDN. Their own privacy policy applies to that service.
  • Google Fonts CDN: We load web fonts from Google Fonts. Google's privacy policy applies.
  • We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

05 Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store personal data, the typical data subject rights (access, correction, deletion) are inherently fulfilled. However, you may:

  • Clear your browser's localStorage to remove your theme preference.
  • Contact us at dasouvik122005@gmail.com with any privacy-related questions or concerns.

06 Open Source Transparency

ScamShield is fully open source. You can audit our entire codebase, including server-side data handling, on our GitHub repository. Transparency is a core principle of this project.

07 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
